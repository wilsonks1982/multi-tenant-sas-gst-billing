# GST Billing SaaS — Business Plan

## Vision

We are building the operating system for compliant business billing in India.

Our mission is to make GST billing, tax-ready invoicing, tenant-led business operations, and financial compliance radically simpler for small and growing businesses across India. We believe billing software should not just generate invoices — it should help businesses get paid faster, stay compliant automatically, and grow confidently.

This company is being built as a multi-tenant SaaS platform focused on Indian business realities:
- GSTIN-based identity
- tax-compliant invoicing
- invoice-to-payment workflows
- role-based business operations
- business administration across companies and tenants
- scalable SaaS billing for the next generation of Indian SMEs

---

## Problem

India has millions of GST-registered businesses, yet the billing stack for many of them is still fragmented, outdated, expensive, or overly complex.

### Pain points in the current market
- GST billing is often done in clunky desktop software or Excel-led workflows
- compliance and operations are disconnected
- invoicing, collections, and user access are not managed in one place
- multi-company businesses struggle with permissions and centralized control
- SaaS tools built for global markets often do not match Indian GST and tax workflows
- many small businesses need something simpler than ERP systems, but stronger than standalone invoice generators

### Market gap
There is a large gap between:
- **basic invoice tools**, and
- **heavy enterprise accounting/ERP platforms**

We fill that gap with a modern, cloud-native, multi-tenant India-first billing platform.

---

## Solution

GST Billing SaaS is a multi-tenant application for Indian businesses to manage:

- GST-compliant invoices
- company-level business billing
- user and role-based access
- tenant and platform administration
- subscription billing for SaaS customers
- audit logging and compliance visibility
- operational dashboards for platform and customer admins

### Core product layers
1. **Tenant onboarding**
   - create tenant workspace
   - onboard company
   - onboard tenant admin
   - establish company access

2. **Billing operations**
   - invoice generation
   - tax breakdown support
   - B2B / B2C support
   - GST-ready records

3. **Business administration**
   - user access per company
   - role switching across companies
   - audit logs and security controls

4. **Platform monetization**
   - subscription plans
   - tenant billing
   - plan upgrades
   - SaaS invoice and renewal management

## Business Model

We are a B2B SaaS company with recurring subscription revenue.

### Revenue streams

#### 1. Subscription plans
Core monetization will come from monthly and annual plans.

Example pricing model:

- **Starter** — ₹2,499/month  
  For single-company small businesses

- **Growth** — ₹4,999/month  
  For growing teams with more billing volume and more users

- **Business** — ₹7,499/month  
  For multi-user, multi-company operational workflows

- **Enterprise** — custom / ₹15,000+ per month  
  For large customers needing advanced admin, priority support, and integrations

#### 2. Annual prepay plans
Annual contracts improve cash flow and reduce churn.

#### 3. Add-ons
Over time:
- extra users
- extra invoice volume
- premium reporting
- compliance modules
- accountant seats
- API access
- branded invoice templates

#### 4. Services revenue
Selective onboarding, migration, implementation, and training services for larger accounts.
