# GST Billing SaaS — One Page Startup Summary

## What We Are Building

GST Billing SaaS is a cloud-based, multi-tenant billing and business administration platform for Indian small and medium businesses.

It is designed to help businesses manage:
- GST-compliant billing
- tenant and company administration
- user access and role control
- subscription billing visibility
- audit logs
- operational dashboards

The platform is positioned between simple invoice generators and heavyweight ERP/accounting systems.

---

## Problem

Many Indian businesses still rely on fragmented workflows for billing and admin operations:
- spreadsheets
- desktop software
- disconnected manual processes
- weak user control
- poor audit visibility

Existing tools are often either:
- too basic for growing businesses, or
- too complex and heavy for small teams

This creates a practical gap in the market for a focused, modern, India-first billing platform.

---

## Our Solution

We are building a product that provides:
- structured tenant onboarding
- company and user management
- company-based access control
- secure admin workflows
- tenant billing oversight
- dashboards for operations and billing visibility
- audit-friendly activity tracking

The product is intended to be simple enough for SMEs, but structured enough for serious business use.

---

## Target Customer

### Initial customer profile
- GST-registered Indian SMEs
- 5 to 50 employees
- service firms, distributors, agencies, small manufacturers
- businesses using manual or semi-manual billing/admin workflows
- customers who need more than invoicing, but less than ERP complexity

### Early buyer profile
- founder-led businesses
- operations-led businesses
- finance/admin-sensitive teams
- customers with real pain around billing clarity and admin control

---

## Business Model

This is a SaaS subscription business.

### Revenue model
Primary revenue will come from monthly and annual subscription plans.

Illustrative pricing direction:
- Starter: ₹1,499–₹2,499/month
- Growth: ₹3,999–₹4,999/month
- Business: ₹6,999–₹7,999/month
- Enterprise: custom

### Expansion potential
Over time, revenue can expand through:
- more users
- more companies
- higher plans
- annual prepay
- premium reporting/admin features
- onboarding or migration services
- integrations

---

## Why This Can Work

The business has a reasonable foundation because:

1. **Billing is a recurring need**  
   Businesses need billing and admin systems continuously, not occasionally.

2. **Operational workflows create stickiness**  
   Once billing, users, and access controls are embedded into operations, switching becomes harder.

3. **Customer value can expand over time**  
   Growing businesses often need more users, more control, more reporting, and better billing workflows.

This creates a practical recurring revenue opportunity.
